Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment? +

Simply click the "Book Experience" or "Book Appointment" buttons throughout our site. You will be redirected immediately to our WhatsApp booking chat (+91 98116 30659), where our concierge will coordinate dates, stylists, and categories with you instantly.

What luxury brands do you use? +

We believe in high-fashion results without compromising hair and skin integrity. We exclusively use authentic international products, including L'Oréal Professionnel, Kérastase Paris, Balmain Hair Couture, Olaplex, Huda Beauty, MAC cosmetics, and premium organic skin serums.

Do you offer bridal makeup trials? +

Yes, we do. Our Chief Bridal Artist Neha Aneja offers dedicated pre-wedding bridal makeup trials. This ensures alignment on style preferences (Dewy, HD, Airbrush, or Royal Traditional), heavy draping, and hair sculpting before the sacred wedding day.

What are your operating hours and branches? +

Our flagship Gurgaon branches have the following schedules:
- Satya The Hive Mall (Sector 102): G-83, Ground Floor. Open 10:00 AM – 9:00 PM daily.
- Sector 109 Branch: Plot No. 16. Open 10:30 AM – 9:00 PM daily.

How do Opulence Memberships work? +

Our elite Silver, Gold, and Platinum Club memberships offer upfront credits (e.g. paying ₹30,000 gives you ₹38,000 in services value) along with structural benefits like up to 20% off retail cosmetics, free monthly cleanups, and priority booking slots.
