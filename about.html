The Founder

Neha Aneja's Vision

Founded in Gurgaon, Opulence Luxury Unisex Salon was born out of a desire to create a sanctuary where high-fashion grooming aligns with holistic wellness. Our founder, Neha Aneja, envisioned a brand that breaks away from formulaic beauty treatments, offering bespoke couture consulting to every client.

Every cut, highlight, facial, and nail extension at Opulence is performed with painstaking attention to detail. We curates only top-tier international brands, ensuring our clients experience the exact standards they would receive in premium luxury spots of London, Dubai, or Beverly Hills.

From the moment you cross our threshold at Satya The Hive Mall, you are treated to a personalized wellness mapping, complimentary gourmet teas, and elite, undivided attention.
